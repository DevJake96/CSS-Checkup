The Legend of Zelda

The legend of Zelda is a video game for the Nintendo console series which released in 1986.

The Legend of Zelda has multiple story lines that take place in different parts of times. It is theorized that the main character, Link, is someone different each iteration of the game. The main plot for the games is the antagonist, Ganon/Ganondorf, kidnaps the princess Zelda, and the protagonist, our hero, Link sets out to rescue her.
